Support

Help keep the music and community work alive.

Alex Parke works as a Jewish and Brazilian artist, musician, educator, researcher, and community builder. Patron support helps sustain the work that happens between concerts: rehearsals, archive research, house concerts, new repertoire, and cultural projects that bring people together.

What Support Makes Possible

Monthly support creates continuity.

This artistic support helps Alex continue his activities as a Jewish artist while also supporting his family, health, and well-being. It makes room for the long-term cultural work that cannot always be sustained by one-off performances alone.

Monthly Newsletter

Patrons receive a monthly newsletter, released on the 30th of every month, with updates, reflections, recordings, repertoire notes, and behind-the-scenes material.

House Concert Invitation

Support also includes an exclusive invitation to intimate house concerts with artists from Alex's circle of klezmer, Brazilian, and world music collaborators.

Community Rehearsals

Patronage helps sustain Bloco Klezmer rehearsals, music direction, and year-round community-building work in Sao Paulo.

Archives & Repertoire

Support gives time and stability to projects like the Honigsberg Archive, the Stuart Manning Archive, and new klezmer repertoire work.
